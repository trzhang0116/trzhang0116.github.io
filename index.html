Research Interests

I am interested in two intertwined areas of machine learning: representation learning and generalization. I believe that a good representation is all we need for human-level generalization and is key to achieving general intelligence.

My recent research primarily focuses on the theoretical foundations of learning world representations and out-of-distribution generalization, as well as translating these theoretical principles into efficient algorithms to solve real-world problems. I also work on reinforcement learning.
